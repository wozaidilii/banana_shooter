# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese internet culture users who come for quick entertainment loops: voting, chatting with revived meme personas, remixing skins, and collecting titles. They are likely on mobile first, browsing casually, and need the interface to feel instant, legible, and meme-native rather than solemn.

## Product Purpose

Cyber Tombstone is a satirical interactive product for revival voting, AI ghost chat, meme skin co-creation, and title collection. Success means users understand the loop quickly, feel the cyber-underworld tone immediately, and can move between voting, chat, skins, and profile without friction.

## Brand Personality

Electric, irreverent, underground. The current direction references cyberpunk anime energy, especially the high-contrast neon city mood of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners, while staying usable as a task-oriented app UI.

## Anti-references

Do not make the app feel like a generic dark SaaS dashboard, a soft glassmorphism template, or a cheap neon arcade skin. Avoid illegible glow-heavy text, decorative effects that obscure controls, and parody visuals that overpower the core voting/chat workflows.

## Design Principles

- Make the revival loop obvious within seconds.
- Let the cyberpunk atmosphere come from surfaces, contrast, motion, and language, not from unreadable decoration.
- Keep repeated controls consistent across voting, chat, skins, and profile.
- Use punchy moments sparingly so the app still feels fast and operable on mobile.
- Treat accessibility as part of the aesthetic: readable contrast, visible focus, touch-safe controls, and reduced-motion support.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve browser zoom, support keyboard focus states, maintain minimum 44px touch targets for primary actions, and provide reduced-motion alternatives for glitch, scanline, hover, and reveal effects.
